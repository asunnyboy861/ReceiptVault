## Description
ReceiptVault is the privacy-first receipt scanner that keeps your financial data exactly where it belongs - on your device.

Tired of receipt apps that force cloud uploads, demand monthly subscriptions, or require account creation just to scan a receipt? ReceiptVault does things differently. Every scan, every receipt, every piece of data stays on your iPhone. No servers. No tracking. No surprises.

INSTANT OCR SCANNING
Point your camera at any receipt and watch the magic happen. ReceiptVault uses Apple's native Vision framework to automatically extract amounts, dates, merchant names, addresses, and phone numbers. No cloud processing, no waiting - results appear in seconds.

ORGANIZE YOUR WAY
Sort receipts by date, amount, or merchant. Search across all fields instantly. Assign categories with 10 preset options or create your own. Add notes to any receipt for context. Find any receipt in under a second.

STATISTICS AT A GLANCE
See where your money goes with beautiful charts. Monthly and yearly spending breakdowns. Category pie charts and merchant rankings. All computed on-device, all updating in real-time.

EXPORT FREELY
Export your receipt data to CSV for spreadsheets or PDF for records. Filter by date range or category before exporting. Share via the system share sheet to any app. No paywall, no limits.

PROTECT YOUR DATA
Lock ReceiptVault with Face ID or Touch ID. Your receipts stay private even if someone picks up your phone. Biometric authentication is optional and easy to toggle.

MULTI-CURRENCY SUPPORT
Track receipts in 15 different currencies. Perfect for travelers and international business. Stats are grouped by currency so your numbers always make sense.

DUPLICATE DETECTION
Accidentally scan the same receipt twice? ReceiptVault warns you before saving a duplicate. No more double-counting expenses.

BACKUP AND RESTORE
Export your entire receipt database as a JSON file. Restore from backup anytime. Your data, your control, always.
